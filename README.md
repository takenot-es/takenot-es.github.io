# Ryan Underwood — Design Portfolio

**Live site:** [takenot-es.github.io](https://takenot-es.github.io)

This repository powers the personal portfolio of Ryan Underwood, a designer, creative director, and systems thinker based in the Chicago area. The site showcases selected case studies spanning brand identity, art direction, design systems, and front-end development.

---

## About Ryan

Ryan Underwood is a senior-level designer and creative leader with a background in healthcare, benefits, and financial services marketing. He specializes in translating complex or data-heavy subjects into clear, compelling visual identities and scalable design systems.

Currently leading design initiatives at Alight Solutions, one of the country's largest benefits providers, Ryan has directed brand work across digital, social, and print channels — and built the centralized design system used across the organization.
